About AI Menu Planner

AI Menu Planner is your gateway to discovering authentic African cuisine. Our platform combines traditional recipes with artificial intelligence to provide you with personalized meal recommendations and detailed cooking instructions.

Whether you're a culinary enthusiast looking to explore new flavors or someone seeking inspiration for your next meal, our AI-powered recommendations are designed to introduce you to the rich and diverse world of African cuisine.
